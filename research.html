RESEARCH

Main Topics that I worked on

Machine Learning for VERITAS Data Analysis

  • Developed machine learning algorithms to analyze gamma-ray data collected by the VERITAS observatory.
  • Improved classification accuracy for astrophysical sources by implementing advanced feature extraction techniques.
  • Conducted statistical analysis to validate the performance of the models, ensuring robustness and reproducibility.
  • Delivered key insights into the behavior of gamma-ray sources, contributing to collaboration publications.

Development and Commissioning of Advanced Gamma-Ray Telescope Systems

  • Led the design, development, and commissioning of the prototype Schwarzschild-Couder Telescope (pSCT) for the Cherenkov Telescope Array.
  • Aligned and validated the optical system, achieving on-axis optical design goals.
  • Managed hardware and software integration, including mirror calibration and alignment tools.
  • Published and presented extensively on the technical and scientific performance of the pSCT, including the detection of the Crab Nebula.
  • Earned the Simon Swordy Outstanding VERITAS Contribution Award in recognition of exceptional contributions.
